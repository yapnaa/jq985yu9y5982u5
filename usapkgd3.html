ITINERARY


    Day 1


Denver - Cheyenne


Take a pleasant flight to Denver, the State Capital of Colorado. Please meet our friendly guide at Baggage Claim #6 located inside the East Terminal by 1:30PM. Drive to the State House of Colorado and Civic Center for sightseeing. Proceed to 16st Street Mall, home to some of Denver’s most historical buildings, and enjoy some free time. Drive to and stay overnight in the State Capitol of Wyoming, City of Cheyenne.

     Red Lion Cheyenne / Clarion Inn Fort Collins (Tours depart on 7/19, 7/20, 7/24, 7/26, 7/27) or similar

    Day 2


Crazy Horse - Mt. Rushmore - Bear Country - Devil’s Tower National Monument - Gillette


Drive to Crazy Horse Indian Memorial this morning and see the world’s largest mountain carving! Created by the hands of both Americans and Natives, the monumental statue stands today in honor of a true hero who fought for freedom, Crazy Horse. America's most famous icon, Mt. Rushmore, is our next stop. George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln look down from their stony height and remind everyone that even the impossible is possible. Drive to Bear Country (own expense) next, discover Native Rocky Mountain Animals up close! Wolves, mountain lions, elk, reindeer, buffalos… and over 160 bears in this largest wild life park! Next drive to the first National Monument in the world, Devil’s Tower! The tower rises over 1,200 feet above the valley, and the hexagonal columns that make up the tower are truly unique and rare, and the tallest and largest in the world! Long known by Native Americans as “Bear’s Dwelling”, the tower is also featured in famous movie “Close Encounters of the Third Kind”. Stay overnight in Gillette, Wyoming.

Activity fees:
Bear Country USA : $15.00/person

    Breakfast is included.

     Ramada Plaza Gillette or similar

    Day 3


Rocky Mountains - Yellowstone National Park - Cody


Drive through the Rockies this morning, enjoy the beautiful scenery along the way! Endless stretch of mountains decorated by glaciers and snow, blooming flowers and lush forests, rivers and waterfalls, outline the picturesque view! Try to spot wild animals from your deluxe coach! Arrive in Yellowstone National Park – World’s First National Park at noon. Yellowstone is home to world’s largest super volcano and over 10,000 geothermal features. Here you can find almost everything you expect to see in a national park! Snow covered mountains, waterfalls, lakes, rivers, canyons, meadows, herds of buffalos, elks… and the most amazing geothermal features in the world! Geysers, hot springs, fumaroles and mud pots are the wonders that make YS truly unique. Arrive at the famous cowboy town of Cody in the afternoon. You may enjoy authentic Cowboy Rodeo (only available June through August) or firearms experience this evening (both at your own expense). Stay overnight in Cody.

Activity fees:
Cody cowboy Rodeo : $25.00/person
Cody firearms experience : $74.00/person

    Breakfast is included

     Buffalo Bill (Cabins) Village / Holiday Inn / Comfort Inn / Best Western Cody or similar

    Day 4


Yellowstone National Park


Enjoy a full day adventure in World’s First National Park - Yellowstone! First of its kind, Yellowstone is the home to the world’s largest super volcano and over 10,000 geothermal features. Here you will find everything you expect to see in a national park: snow covered mountains, waterfalls, lakes, meadows, herds of buffalo, and elks. Not to mention the scenery such as geysers, hot springs, fumaroles and mud pots that makes Yellowstone truly unique. Be sure to see Grand Prismatic Spring, Fountain Paint Pots, Old Faithful Geyser, Yellowstone Grand Canyon, Yellowstone River, and Norris Geyser Basin. Stay overnight in Cody.

Activity fees:
Yellowstone National Park + Grand Teton + Mt.Rushmore + Crazy Horse + Devil’s Tower + Great Salt Lake + fuel surcharge : $140.00/person

    Breakfast is included.

     Buffalo Bill (Cabins) Village / Holiday Inn / Comfort Inn / Best Western Cody or similar

    Day 5


Yellowstone - Grand Teton National Park - Jackson - Great Salt Lake - Salt Lake City


Continue to explore Yellowstone National Park this morning. Then travel along the Snake River to Grand Teton National Park, where we see this natural wonder's amazing views. Travel to Jackson, a famous cowboy town as well as a world-renowned vacation destination. Be sure to visit the Elk Antler Arches Square located in the center of Jackson! Arrive in Salt Lake City to visit the Great Salt Lake, known as the America’s Dead Sea. Stay overnight in Salt Lake City.

    Breakfast is included.

     La Quinta Salt Lake Midvale or similar

    Day 6

This morning drive to nearby newly renovated State House of Utah and the renowned Mormon Temple. Then you will be transferred back to hotel at about 10:00a.m. The check out time is 12:00p.m. for this hotel. You have 2 choices for getting to Las Vegas: 1. Take the free shuttle bus You can take the free shttle bus from Salt Lake City to Las Vegas. Please note it will take you 6 hours driving 420 miles to get to Las Vegas. When you arrive in Las Vegas, we'll directly send you to the hotel to save your money! 2. Take the airplane by yourself Please book your flights from Salt Lake City to Las Vegas after 12:00p.m. We recommend the hotel free shuttle bus for you. When you arrive in Las Vegas 1. You can head to Park MGM Las Vegas by yourself. We recommend a taxi service. Taxi can be arranged for USD 40. Please contact your guide by calling the phone number on your E-ticket. 2. If you depart from Salt Lake City at 12:00p.m., then you will arrive in Las Vegas at 12:20p.m., due to one hour time differences. You can join the tour at the Las Vegas Buffet (own expense, subject to availability) between 12:00p.m.-2:00p.m. (4300 E. Sunset Rd., C - 1, Henderson, NV 89014).

Activity fees:
Las Vegas High roller : $40.00/person
Las Vegas City Trip :$35.00/person

     Park MGM Las Vegas or similar

    Day 7


Las Vegas - Grand Canyon South - Hoover Dam - Las Vegas


In the morning, we'll proceed to one of the Seven Wonders of the Natural World - Grand Canyon National Park! The Grand Canyon continues to amaze visitors year after year with its size and beauty. Afterwards, we'll travel to see the magnificent Hoover Dam before heading back to Las Vegas. You may take an optional night tour to explore the prosperity of nightlife in Las Vegas or try your luck in one of the many casinos (own expense, subject to availability).
If the farm is not open we will visit Merrimack Premium Outlets instead.

Activity fees:
Grand Canyon National Park : $90.00/person

Skywalk : $25.00/Person

    Breakfast is included.

     Park MGM Las Vegas or similar

    Day 8


Las Vegas Free Day / Antelope Canyon and Horseshoe Bend


You will have these options today. Please select one of them when booking. Option 1: Antelope Canyon and Horseshoe Bend Tour: In the morning, we will traveling across the Antelope Canyon and Horseshoe Bend, which is one of the world's ten photography site. Then we will travel across Glen Canyon Dam of North America's second largest man-made lake, Lake Powell. We'll then return to Las Vegas. Las Vegas is constantly changing and always exciting. Take an optional city tour of Las Vegas and explore the "Entertainment Capital of the World" or attend one of the unique shows that are exclusively performed in Las Vegas. * Please DO confirm the number of the guide before departure. Option 2: Free day in Las Vegas: Explore Las Vegas at your own leisure today, and enjoy some free time at one of the most entertaining cities in the U.S.!

    Breakfast is included.

     Park MGM Las Vegas or similar

    Day 9


Las Vegas - Ethel M Chocolate Factory - Tanger Outlets - Los Angeles


You will depart from your hotel in the morning and head to the Ethel M Chocolate Factory and Botanical Cactus Garden just outside of the Strip. Ethel M Chocolate Factory and Cactus Gardens is quite the adventure. It attracts millions of tourists a year. You can see chocolates being made, sample gourmet treats and meander through the maze of paths at the Botanical Cactus Gardens at no charge. You'll enjoy the optional buffet brunch in Las Vegas and depart from Las Vegas after. On the way back to Los Angeles you'll stop by the famous Tanger Outlet Center where you'll be able to satisfy all your shopping needs. You will arrive in Los Angeles by the evening.

Activity fees:
Las Vegas Buffet Lunch : $16.99/person

    Breakfast is included.
