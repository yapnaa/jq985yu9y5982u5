Dubai

Dubai is a stirring alchemy of profound traditions and ambitious futuristic vision wrapped into starkly evocative desert splendour.
It's hard not to admire Dubai for its indefatigable verve, ambition and ability to dream up and realise projects that elsewhere would never get off the drawing board. This is a superlative-craving society that has birthed audaciously high buildings and palm-shaped islands. Sci-fi concepts such as flying taxis, a lightning-fast Hyperloop train and an army of robocops are all reflections of a mindset that fearlessly embraces the future.

ITINERARY


    Day 1


Dubai


Welcome to Dubai, on arrival you will welcomed by our representative and transferred to your hotel. You are free to explore the city on your today.
Later in evening, proceed for Dinner Dhow Cruise which offers a fascinating glimpse of the radiance of Dubai at night .Dinner cruises unfold the shimmering high-rise structures that surround the creek, whilst sailing under bridges and in view of marvelous architectures such as the National Bank of Dubai, Chamber of Commerce Building and much more. The dinner cruise from 8:30 PM to 10:30 PM. Relish all-inclusive sumptuous buffet dinner, soft drinks, tea and lots more in our 2-hour Dhow Dinner Cruise.

    Dinner is included.

     FOUR POINTS BY SHERATON HOTEL or similar

    Day 2


Dubai


After breakfast, we will take you for half day city tour of Dubai, where you will experience lot of activities.
Our tour begins with a drive into the Palm Jumeirah dubbed as the eighth wonder of the world and head for a quick photostop in front of the Atlantis Hotel. We then drive towards the beautiful Jumeirah Mosque built in the year 1979 in the tradition of the fatimi mosques of Egypt. Our tour is headed towards the famous Sheikh Zayed road highway to view Burj Khalifa Tower, the tallest building in the world from a long distance. Our journey continues towards the old part of the city where we pass impressive palaces of the ruling family of Dubai on our way to the famous Dubai Creek. We then pay a visit to the famous gold and spice souk where bargains galore await. Then you will be taken to, Burj Khalifa in a shared vehicle. We hope you enjoy the panoramic view from the 124th floor of the most vibrant and buzzing city in the world!
Later in the day, experience the amazing Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo Ticket with return transfer

    Breakfast is included.

     FOUR POINTS BY SHERATON HOTEL or similar

    Day 3


Dubai


Today after breakfast, spend a day experiencing the thrill of Aqua venture and watching the underwater world at the Lost Chambers within the remarkable Atlantis, The Palm resort. Take part in water slides and rides, including the world’s 1st dual water slide within a waterslide, across this 42-acre aqua haven - Aqua venture. Race through tidal river rapids, battle the King of the Sea at the Tower of Poseidon, and ride through the dark and winding Aquaconda water tunnel. Take a plunge more than 27 meters down the Leap of Faith and treasure the opportunity to get up-close-and-personal with sharks and rays during Ziggurat ride. If not an adventure enthusiast then maybe you could glide effortlessly along a lazy river ride, or simply kick back with a book on the beautiful private beach. Continue your adventure at the Lost Chambers. Explore the lost city of Atlantis, once known as the world’s most advanced civilizations. Make your way through the ten various themed chambers showcasing over 21 species of marine exhibits from all over the world. In this awe-inspiring sea world, you’ll come face-to-fin with sharks, piranhas, illuminated jellyfish, seahorses and much more.

    Breakfast is included

Dubai
