ITINERARY


    Day 1


Costa del Sol


When you arrive in the stunning Costa del Sol, we'll welcome you at the airport and transfer you to the hotel. A driver will be waiting for you at the airport holding a sign with your name on it and the name of the local provider. Flight details are required when booking. The maximum waiting time is 1 hour after flight arrival. If no airport pick-up is needed, we can also pick you up from train station or any hotel in Malaga and transfer you to the hotel. If you'd like to request this, please specify the full address and requested pick-up time. You'll have the rest of the day at your leisure and we'll have dinner at the hotel.

     Melia Costa del Sol Sol Don Marco Sol Don Pablo or similar

    Day 2


Costa del Sol


After breakfast at the hotel, we'll embark on a morning panoramic city tour of Malaga - capital city of the Costa del Sol. We'll see the most important monuments and avenues in the city. You'll have the afternoon at your leisure to enjoy the wonderful beaches and surrounding areas before dinner at the hotel. Tips: You'll be free to explore the beautiful city of Malaga with the help of our knowledgeable driver.

    Breakfast is included.

     Melia Costa del Sol Sol Don Marco Sol Don Pablo or similar

    Day 3


Costa del Sol - Tarifa - Tangier - Rabat - Casablanca


We'll have breakfast at the hotel before we journey on the picturesque tourist route of Costa del Sol passing through the gorgeous destinations of Marbella and Puerto Banus to arrive in Tarifa. We'll then board a ferry to cross the Strait of Gibraltar to reach Africa. After arriving in Tangier, we'll disembark, clear customs and drive southward via Larache to Rabat. This diplomatic and administrative capital is the official residence of the King Mohamed VI. We'll go on a Rabat City Tour visiting the Royal Palace (outside), Mausoleum of Mohammed V and the unfinished Hassan Tower. These sites are important examples of Almohade architecture. Afterwards, we'll head to Casablanca, a city made famous by the classic Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman 1942 romance film "Casablanca." You'll have time to yourself to explore Morocco's largest city! We'll then have dinner at the hotel.

    Breakfast is included.

     Prince de Paris (tourist class) Golden Tulip (first class) From August 2019: Kenzi Basmas (tourist class) Farah Casablanca (first class) or similar

    Day 4


Casablanca - Marrakesh


After having breakfast at the hotel, we'll take a panoramic city tour of Casablanca showing you Anfa Boulevard, United Nations Square and Hassan II Mosque (Outside). We'll then travel to Marrakesh eating lunch en route. This Moroccan city is located within an extensive oasis between the Mid-Atlas Mountains and the pre-desertic area. We'll then embark on a city sightseeing tour to see Koutoubia Tower, Saadian Tombs and El Bahia Palace. We'll next tour the medina, souks and the magic Jamaa el Fna Square, which is packed with fortune tellers, snake tamers, acrobats and dancers. There will be an optional dinner with show at Fantasia Chez Ali (own expense).

    Breakfast is included.

     Almas Atlas Asni From August 2019: Almas (tourist class), Atlas Asni (first class) or similar

    Day 5


Marrakesh - Meknes - Fez


After breakfast, we'll head northeast to Meknes - an old walled Imperial City located in the heart of the Mid Atlas. Here, we'll take a panoramic city tour and stop to admire the great Bab Al Mansour Gate. It's Moroccoâ€™s most beautiful gate and the most well known monument in Meknes. The tour will then travel to Fez, another one of the Imperial Cities. Its University of al-Karaouine is the largest Islamic sanctuary in Morocco. We'll have dinner at the hotel.

    Breakfast is included.

     Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class), Parc Palace (first class) From August 2019: Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class), Zalagh Parc Palace (tourist class), Zalagh Parc Palace (first class) or similar

    Day 6


Fez


During the morning, we'll see more of Fez including landmarks such as Seven Gates of the Royal Palace, Jewish Quarter or Mellah and Gate of Bab Bou Jelous. We'll also take a walking tour of the Medina or Old City, declared UNESCO World Heritage Site. Afterwards, you'll have the afternoon free to further explore Fez. Breakfast and lunch will be provided at the hotel. There will be an optional dinner and show offered at an additional cost.

    Breakfast is included.

     Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class), Parc Palace (first class) From August 2019: Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class), Zalagh Parc Palace (tourist class), Zalagh Parc Palace (first class) or similar

    Day 7


Fez - Tangier - Tarifa - Costa del Sol


We'll have breakfast at the hotel and then head back to Tangier. We'll transfer to the pier to board the ferry to return across the Strait of Gibraltar back to Spain. Once in Spain, we'll drive back to Costa del Sol. We'll have dinner and spend the night on the Costa del Sol.

    Breakfast is included.

     Melia Costa del Sol Sol Don Marco Sol Don Pablo or similar

    Day 8


Costa del Sol


Your arrangements conclude today. We trust you enjoyed your Vacation!.After breakfast at the hotel, we'll transfer you to the airport/train station.

    Breakfast is included.
