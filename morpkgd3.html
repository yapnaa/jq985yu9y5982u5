ITINERARY


    Day 1


Costa del Sol


When you arrive in the stunning Costa del Sol, we'll welcome you at the airport and transfer you to the hotel. A driver will be waiting for you at the airport holding a sign with your name on it and the name of the local provider. Flight details are required when booking. The maximum waiting time is 1 hour after flight arrival. If no airport pick-up is needed, we can also pick you up from train station or any hotel in Malaga and transfer you to the hotel. If you'd like to request this, please specify the full address and requested pick-up time. You'll have the rest of the day at your leisure and we'll have dinner at the hotel.

     Melia Costa del Sol Sol Don Marco Sol Don Pablo or similar

    Day 2


Costa del Sol


After breakfast at the hotel, we'll embark on a morning panoramic city tour of Malaga - capital city of the Costa del Sol. We'll see the most important monuments and avenues in the city. You'll have the afternoon at your leisure to enjoy the wonderful beaches and surrounding areas before dinner at the hotel. Tips: You'll be free to explore the beautiful city of Malaga with the help of our knowledgeable driver.

    Breakfast is included.

     Melia Costa del Sol Sol Don Marco Sol Don Pablo or similar

    Day 3


Costa del Sol - Fez


We'll eat breakfast at the hotel before we travel via the picturesque tourist route of Costa del Sol through internationally renowned places such as Marbella and Puerto Banus. When we arrive in Tarifa, there will be a ferry boat waiting to take us across the Strait of Gibraltar to Africa. We'll then arrive in Morocco, disembark, clear customs and continue on by bus via Larache to Fez. Fez's vehicle-free medina is one of the world's best preserved old cities and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. We'll have dinner at the hotel

    Breakfast is included.

     Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class) Zalagh Parc Palace (tourist class) Ramada (first class) Zalagh Parc Palace (first class) or similar

    Day 4


Fez


During the morning, we'll see more of Fez including landmarks such as Seven Gates of the Royal Palace, Jewish Quarter, and Gate of Bab Bou Jelous. We'll also take a walking tour of the Medina or Old City, declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Afterwards, you'll have the afternoon free to further explore Fez. Breakfast and lunch will be provided at the hotel. There will be an optional dinner and show offered at an additional cost..

    Breakfast is included.

     Menzeh Zalagh (tourist class) Zalagh Parc Palace (tourist class) Ramada (first class) Zalagh Parc Palace (first class) or similar

    Day 5


Fez - Meknes - Marrakesh


After breakfast at the hotel, we'll drive toward the ancient city of Meknes - located in the heart of the Mid Atlas. We'll admire the great Bab Al Mansour Gate, arguably Morocco's most beautiful. Afterwards, we'll continue driving southward through the Mid-Atlas mountains to Marrakesh, located within an extensive oasis between the Atlas Mountains and the pre-desertic area. We'll then have dinner at the hotel in Marrakesh.

    Breakfast is included.

     Atlas Asni (tourist class) Atlas Medina (first class) or similar

    Day 6


Marrakesh


We'll have breakfast at the hotel. Our Marrakesh sightseeing tour includes stops at the Koutoubia Mosque, Saadian Tombs and El Bahia Palace. Lunch will also be provided. In the afternoon, we'll walk around the medina, souks and the magic Jamaa el Fna Square, which is packed with fortune tellers, snake tamers, acrobats and dancers. Donâ€™t forget your camera! We'll then go back to the hotel and you'll have free time to yourself. There will be an optional dinner with show at Fantasia Chez Ali (own expense).

    Breakfast is included.

     Atlas Asni (tourist class) Atlas Medina (first class) or similar

    Day 7


Marrakesh - Casablanca - Rabat


After breakfast, we'll depart north via Benguerir and Settat to Casablanca. This famous destination is the industrial and economic capital and the most populated city in Morocco, home to ten percent of the country's population. We'll take a panoramic tour through the most interesting sections of the city including Anfa Boulevard, United Nations Square, and the exterior of Hassan II Mosque. We'll depart in the afternoon. Our next destination: Rabat, the diplomatic and administrative capital and the official residence of the King Mohamed VI. We'll have dinner and hotel accommodations in Rabat.

    Breakfast is included.

     Golden Tulip From August 2019: Chellah (tourist class) Farah Rabat (tourist class) Rabat (first class) Farah Rabat (first class) or similar

    Day 8


Rabat - Larache - Tangier


We'll start the morning with breakfast at the hotel. Then, we'll take a morning city tour visiting the important sites of Rabat such as the Royal Palace (outside), Mausoleum of Mohammed V and the unfinished Hassan Tower. During the afternoon, we will travel through Larache to reach Tangier, a cosmopolitan city situated at the entrance of the Strait of Gibraltar. We'll take a short panoramic tour; then you'll have free time to explore on your own. We'll have dinner and accommodations at the hotel.

    Breakfast is included.

     Hotel Hilton Hotel Kenzi Solazur From August 2019: Hilton Garden Inn (tourist class) Hilton City Center (first class) or similar

    Day 9


Tangier - Costa del Sol


We'll have breakfast at the hotel before our transfer to the pier to board the ferry to return across the Strait of Gibraltar back to Spain. Once in Spain, we'll drive return to the Costa del Sol to stay for the night.

    Breakfast is included.

     Melia Costa del Sol (tourist class) Sol Don Marco (first class) Sol Don Pablo (first class) From August 2019: Melia Costa del Sol Ibersol Alay or similar

    Day 10


Costa del Sol


Your arrangements conclude today. We trust you enjoyed your Vacation!.After breakfast at the hotel, we'll transfer you to the airport/train station.

    Breakfast is included.
