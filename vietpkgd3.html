Vietnam

VVietnam is a thin strip of land in Southeast Asia, bordered by Cambodia, Laos and the South China Sea. Known for its buzzing cities, spectacular beaches and incredible street food, this country is very popular in the backpacking circuit
Vietnam is brought to life by the myriad magical experiences it offers. Its cities are full of life with happy locals zip-zapping on colourful scooters, the countryside offers a landscape that is unforgettable, Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park has an impressive network of caves to explore.


Overview


  •  

    Walk around Phong Nha - Ke Bang national park. Explore Hoi An and My Son Sanctuary, two of the World’s Heritage Site. Visit Khai Dinh Tomb – the combination of Asian and European cultural structure, embodying the beauty of this ancient city. See Thien Mu Pagoda – the most beautiful pagoda of Hue, which is considered as the National pagoda of The Nguyen Dynasty


Tour Code : 499471
Duration : 6 days
Departure City : Danang
End City : Hue
Language : English

ITINERARY


    Day 1


Danang - Hoian Old Town


We will pick you up at Danang airport depending on your arrival time (pick-up time is from 8:00 - 14:00). Transfer to Hoi An old town and check in the hotel. Arrive in Hoi An - one of the World’s Heritage Site. You have the chance to walk around this town, visit the ancient houses, ancient assembly hall, ancestral temple, and the symbol of Hoi An, the Japanese Bridge Pagoda. In Hoi An, everything is preserved as they were hundreds of years ago. At night time, Hoi An shows its beauty with colorful lanterns hung all around the town, and in the night market, where you can buy various kinds of souvenirs. (own expense) Overnight in HoiAn

     Hotel 3 star - Standard room or similar

    Day 2


Hoian - My Son


Have breakfast at hotel in the morning. Here's your itinerary for the day: 08:30 am: The tour guide will pick you up at the hotel. We will visit My Son Sanctuary – one of World’s Heritage Sites. 10:00 am: My Son Sanctuary, which used to be the citadel of Champa Kingdom, has been keeping the ancient beauty with more than 70 architectural works, which are the temples of the old Champa Kingdom. They still survive until today, despite the destruction of many wars. You will not only discover the old Cham Pa monuments but also admire the Champa traditional dance performed by the professional local artists. 12:30 pm: Depart for a restaurant near Hoi An. 01:30 pm: Have lunch in a local restaurant. 02:30 pm: We will go back to your hotel in Hoi An. Overnight in HoiAn

    Breakfast is included.

     Hotel 3 star - Standard room or similar

    Day 3


Traque Village - Danang


Check out of the hotel after breakfast. 08:00 am: The local guide will see you at your hotel with your bicycle. You will cycle to a local farm in Tra Que Village. A short stroll will take you around the vegetable garden and then you can join the farmers in preparing the land and fertilizing it with seaweed from the local lake. The following steps are raking the ground, sowing, watering, picking vegetable, and many other gardening activities. After all these activities, you will take a short rest and enjoy a foot bath before preparing lunch with a local family. Your lunch will consist of many local delicacies including “Tam Huu” spring roll, “Banh Xeo” or local pancake from the people in the middle region of Vietnam. You can join the cooking with the chef on these meals. After your morning excursions and your lunch, you will take a Vietnamese traditional post-prandial nap before learning how to harvest and wrap the vegetables. Around 2 pm, bid farewell to your host family before riding back to Hoi An. We will transfer you to your hotel in Da nang. Overnight in Da nang.

    Breakfast is included

     Hotel 3 star - Standard room or similar

    Day 4


Danang - Hue Emperial City


Enjoy breakfast at the hotel and then check out. 7:30 am: In the morning, the tour guide will pick you up at the hotel then we start the Hue City Tour. On the way to Hue, you will pass through Hai Van tunnel, the longest tunnel in Southeast Asia. Besides that, you will have some time to enjoy the golden sandy beach of Lang Co Bay – one of the most beautiful bay in the planet. 9:30 am: After arriving in Hue citadel, you will visit Khai Dinh Tomb – the combination of Asian and European cultural structure, embodying the beauty of this ancient city. Thien Mu Pagoda, the most beautiful pagoda of Hue, is considered as the National pagoda of The Nguyen Dynasty, and is your next destination after Khai Dinh Tomb. You can find the relics of Monk Thich Quang Duc and his private car. He is an eloquent witness of the Buddhist era in Vietnam. 12:30 pm: At lunch time, we'll introduce to you Hue specialties – the featured local dishes of this ancient city, in one of Hue’s famous restaurants. 01:30 pm: We will take you to Hue Imperial city, the former administrative center as well as the accommodation of 13 Kings of the Nguyen Dynasty – the last feudatory Empire of Vietnam. The Imperial city is preserved as new for hundreds years, and tourists are allowed to visit many places such as the Meridian Gate, Thai Hoa Palace, and Duyet Thi duong theater. Our tour guide will tell you the secret stories of the Royal family, of the Kings and the Queens’ lives. After that, we will drop you off at Hue hotel to check in. Overnight in Hue

    Breakfast is included.

     Hotel 3 star - Standard room or similar

    Day 5


Phong Nha Cave


In the morning: 6:30: Our guide and bus will pick you up at your hotel. On the way, stop to visit La Vang church and our guide will present Quang Tri Citadel (Nonstop) which was built in 1824 (the fierce battle of 81 days and nights has made this citadel well-known all over the world). Then to continue the itinerary and our guide will introduce many places such as: Ben Hai river, Hien Luong bridge, and Doc Mieu base. 12:00 PM: Arrive in Phong Nha - Ke Bang national Park. Lunch at a restaurant. After lunch, cruise along the Son river to Phong Nha Cave. You will feel the magical and magnificent caves. Phong Nha also boasts underground rivers, large caverns and passageways and broad, pristine sandbanks. The most impressive cave, Phong Nha cave, is probably also the most beautiful with many fascinating rock formations with imaginative names such as The Lion and Fairy Caves. Inside the Phong Nha Caves, the more illusory the stalactites and stalagmites look as they glitter when the right light is shone on them. 3:30 Pm: Back to Hue on Ho Chi Minh trail. On the way back, you have snacks on the bus. 7:30 Pm: We arrive in Hue and drop you off at your hotel.

    Breakfast is included.

     Hotel 3 star - Standard room or similar

    Day 6


Hue Departure


Have breakfast then check out of the hotel. Pick you up and transfer you to Phu Bai airport to catch your next flight. We hope this tour will bring you many unforgettable memories and joyful moments. See you again!

    Breakfast is included.
