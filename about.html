Who We Are


TripShark is extremely spirited about creating inspirational, tailor-made travel plans around the world. We thrive to cater to your wanderlust. Our travel pundits and devoted team are here with shark advices to bring you the best travel plans and destinations. We cover your travel plan from a to z with personalizations based on your choice, leaving you ‘free’ to enjoy your holiday of a lifetime with no gimmicks, no hassle, and no stress.


What makes us different? With highly experienced team providing quality tailor-made customised holidays with an ‘off-the beaten path’ themed approach, we take you to well-known destinations, as well as far flung locations (or a mixture of both), leaving you safe in the knowledge that you will be looked after every step of the way.


Be safeguarded in the knowledge that we go the extra mile to ensure that your holiday is completely stress free. So come and join us for memorable and never ending shark experiences.
