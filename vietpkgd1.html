Vietnam

VVietnam is a thin strip of land in Southeast Asia, bordered by Cambodia, Laos and the South China Sea. Known for its buzzing cities, spectacular beaches and incredible street food, this country is very popular in the backpacking circuit
Vietnam is brought to life by the myriad magical experiences it offers. Its cities are full of life with happy locals zip-zapping on colourful scooters, the countryside offers a landscape that is unforgettable, Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park has an impressive network of caves to explore.

ITINERARY


    Day 1


Hanoi


Our driver will pick you up from the airport and send you to your hotel in Ha Noi center. You will meet with our staff to confirm all the information for your trip. In the afternoon, you have free time to explore. Night 1: Overnight in the hotel in Ha Noi.

     Hanoi Impressive Hotel or similar 3 Star Hotel or similar

    Day 2


Hanoi - Halong Bay


8h00: Pick up from your hotel in Hanoi and depart for Halong Bay. Halong is located in Quang Ninh province, 170 km northeast of Hanoi. (Listed by UNESCO as a natural heritage site in 1994, Halong bay has been a famous tourist destination in Vietnam). Our bus trip will have some stop-over on the way for photo or refreshment. 12h00: Arrive at Halong Bay and board our boat. Lunch will be served on board. Cruise around the bay along famous sites such as: Fighting cock's islet, Stone dog or Joss stick bowl islet. Visit Thien Cung cave, the beautiful stalactites and stalagmites which have been formed millions of years ago. Back to the junk boat and keep cruising to view the fabulous scenery of Halong bay. After a 4-hour boat trip, meet our driver again and transfer back to Hanoi. We will arrive at your hotel at around 19h00 – 19h30. Night 2: Overnight in the hotel in Ha Noi.

    Breakfast is included.

     Hanoi Impressive Hotel or similar 3 Star Hotel or similar

    Day 3


Hanoi - Bus Sapa - Laochai - Tavan - Giang Ta Chai


06h30 – 06h45: Our car and guide will pick you up at the meeting point and depart to Lao Cai, on the highway running from Noi Bai – Lao Cai, 245Km in less than 5 hours. You have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful scenery on both sides of this road. 12h00: Arrive at Sapa. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant then proceed with the tour. 13h00: Our car will take the visitors and start the journey for about 4 hours to visit Lao Chai – Ta Van and Giang Ta Chai. Explore the life of ethnic minorities and admire the spectacular natural scenery with the terraced rice fields. Visit Sapa ancient rock with engraved images on big and small rocks, admire the bridge which is made by clouds and cross the poetic Muong Hoa stream. Trekking information: Length: 4 hours, Duration: 7-10km Night 3: Overnight in the hotel in Sapa town

    Breakfast is included

     3 star hotel or similar

    Day 4


Sapa - Cat cat village - Bus back to Hanoi


09h00: Our driver and guide will take you back to Sapa to rejoin the group and visit Cat Cat village together. 10h00: Explore Cat Cat – Shin chai village of H’mong – a colonial French-discovered area and a renowned resort of officials from the early twentieth century. Arrive at Cat Cat village. Visitors will visit the hydropower station, the waterfall of Cat Cat village, as well as learn about the culture, customs and unique people with traditional crafts such as weaving, carved silver, and hand forged agricultural tools. 12h00: Pick up from Cat Cat village and go back to Sapa town. Enjoy a meal at a local restaurant. Free time to explore or shop some souvenirs. 16h00: Our driver will take you back to Hanoi. Recheck in the hotel in Hanoi. You will arrive in Hanoi at around 21.30 pm. Night 4: Overnight in the hotel in Ha Noi.

    Breakfast is included.

     Hanoi Impressive Hotel or similar 3 Star Hotel or similar

    Day 5


Hanoi - Hoalu - Tam Coc


08h00: Pick up from the hotel/stay. 08h30: Drive to Ninh Binh Province 10h00: Arrive at Hoa Lu Ancient Capital. 10h15: Visit King Dinh and King Le’s temples. 11h30: Arrive in Tam Coc Wharf. 11h45: Have a buffet lunch with Vietnamese cuisine. 13h00: Get on Sampan boats, pass through caves and the countryside. 15h00: Back to Tam Coc Wharf to chill out. 15h15: Bike across the country road and around the village. 16h00: Get on the shuttle bus to go back to Hanoi. Night 5: Overnight in the hotel in Ha Noi.

    Breakfast is included.

     Hanoi Impressive Hotel or similar 3 Star Hotel or similar

Hanoi


Breakfast at the hotel and then you have free time to explore. The driver will pick you up and send you to the airport. This is the end of your trip
