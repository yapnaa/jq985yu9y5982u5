Prague

Prague is the equal of Paris in terms of beauty. Its history goes back a millennium. And the beer? The best in Europe. The 1989 Velvet Revolution that freed the Czechs from communism bequeathed to Europe a gem of a city to stand beside stalwarts such as Rome, Paris and London. Not surprisingly, visitors from around the world have come in droves, and on a hot summer's day it can feel like you’re sharing Charles Bridge with half of humanity. But even the crowds can’t take away from the spectacle of a 14th-century stone bridge, a hilltop castle and a lovely, lazy river – the Vltava – that inspired one of the most hauntingly beautiful pieces of 19th-century classical music, Smetana’s Moldausymphony.

ITINERARY


    Day 1


Prague


Welcome to Prague! Today you'll land in Vaclav Havel Airport Prague (PRG), where a driver will be waiting to take you to your hotel. Our local guide will be at the hotel and will provide you will all the necessary information. Later in the afternoon, we'll provide transport to Prague Old Town Square, a marvelous place to acquaint yourself with this magical city.

     DUO Hotel Pyramida Park Hotel Klosterneuburg or similar

    Day 2


Prague


Today we'll introduce you to the capital of the Czech Republic, and the Baroque jewel of Europe. Our local expert will lead you on an informative city tour, the highlight of which is a visit to Prague Castle, admission included. Afterwards, we'll explore another side of the city on a river cruise. In the afternoon you'll have free time to explore Prague on your own.

    Breakfast is included.

     DUO Hotel Pyramida Park Hotel Klosterneuburg or similar

    Day 3


Prague - Munich - Innsbruck


We'll leave early and travel to Bavaria, arriving in Munich at midday. There we'll visit the futuristic BMW Welt with its fascinating architecture and multi-media exhibitions. After that, you'll be given ample time for lunch (own expense) and a stroll through the historic center. In the afternoon we'll travel to Austria, arriving at Innsbruck in the evening. You will be transferred to the Golden Roof area of Old Town for dinner (own expense).

    Breakfast is included.

     Gasthof Dollinger Alpin Park or similar

    Day 4


Innsbruck - Swarovski Crystal Worlds - Kitzbuhels


Begin your day by exploring the fascinating Swarovski Crystal Worlds. We'll take a walk through the inspiring gardens and learn how pieces of crystal are produced. Back in Innsbruck you'll have some free time to stroll through the historic center of Innsbruck, before we travel between mountains to Kitzbuhel, where you can see this picturesque little village, a famous ski resort where we can enjoy the snow for a large part of the year! Free time. Tips: Dinner included: vegetarian option possible

    Breakfast is included.

     Ibis Salzburg Nord or similar

    Day 5


Kitzbuhel - Salzburg - Vienna


We travel between mountains to Salzburg, our guide accompanies us to the most beautiful streets of the historic centre of this city declared a World Heritage site. We then go on our way between lakes and mountains. A very beautiful scenic stage in the Alps. We stop at St.Wolfgang, a famous place of pilgrimage in Austria. We continue to Traunkirchen along with the very beautiful Traunsee lake, where we include a cruise on the lake to see four castles and disembarking in Gmunden. Continuation to Vienna, arrival at the end of the afternoon.

    Breakfast is included.

     Arion Cityhotel or similar

    Day 6


Vienna - Budapest


We'll depart for Hungary in the morning, arriving in Budapest at lunchtime. Here we'll discover the Queen of the Danube on a panoramic tour. There will be free time at the end of the day in the lively area of Vaci Utca, where there are many little eateries serving various ethnic foods (own expense). From 2019 August: Vienna After breakfast, at around 12.00h, we start with a scenic visit around Vienna with a local expert. Here, we experience its majestic avenues, its palaces and the vibrant city centre. Directly afterwards, we head the Schönbrunn Palace where visitors can stroll around and enjoy its beautiful gardens. In the evening we go to the City Hall Square to enjoy the lighting and the nightlife in the neighbouring streets, where you can dine at your preferred restaurant with cuisines from around the world including Austrian, Chinese, Indian and Italian.

    Breakfast is included.

     Danubius Arena or similar

    Day 7


Budapest


Today we'll enjoy a scenic cruise along the Danube, affording impressive views of the city's skyline and the magnificent parliament building! Afterwards you'll have time to explore on your own and maybe visit a few of the attractions we passed by. From 2019 August: Vienna - Budapest We depart for Hungary. We have a little under a 3-hours trip to Budapest. Arrival to Budapest at around 11.30 hrs. Discover the “Queen of the Danube”on a scenic tour of highlight. This city is divided by the Danube, with historic Buda on one side and cosmopoli-tan “Pest” on the opposite bank. After time for lunch, we will include a boat tour along the Danube River; the views of the city and parliament are impressive. Leisure time after the tour. There will be time at the end of the day in the lively area of Vaci Utca, where there are lots of little restaurants with food from different countries (Indian, Chinese, Hungarian and Italian).

    Breakfast is included.

    

    Day 8


Budapests


The tour concludes after breakfast at the hotel. We wish you a safe journey home and we hope to see you again very soon!

    Breakfast is included.
